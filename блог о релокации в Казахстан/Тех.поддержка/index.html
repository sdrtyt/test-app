Preparation

If you are planning to relocate to Kazakhstan, there are a few things you need to take care of before you go. First, make sure you have a valid passport and visa. You will also need to arrange for health insurance, as medical care can be expensive in Kazakhstan. Additionally, it's a good idea to learn some basic Kazakh or Russian phrases, as these are the most commonly spoken languages in the country.

If you have any questions or concerns about the preparation process, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Transportation

Getting around Kazakhstan can be challenging, especially if you don't speak the language. However, there are several transportation options available, including taxis, buses, and trains. If you plan on driving, be aware that the roads in Kazakhstan can be rough, and traffic laws are not always strictly enforced.

If you need assistance with transportation arrangements, please let us know.

Housing

Finding a place to live in Kazakhstan can be difficult, especially if you are unfamiliar with the area. However, there are several options available, including apartments, houses, and hotels. It's important to consider factors such as location, safety, and affordability when choosing a place to live.

If you need help finding a place to live or have any questions about housing in Kazakhstan, we are here to assist you.
