Author Support

As an author, having a support system is crucial, especially during times of change. Here are some resources that have helped me during my relocation:

  • Meetup: Join a writing group or attend a literary event in your new area to connect with other writers and gain new perspectives.
  • Writer's Digest: This website offers articles, workshops, and other resources to help writers improve their craft, no matter where they are located.
  • Poets & Writers: This organization provides information on grants, awards, and other opportunities for writers, regardless of location.
  • Airbnb: Rent a quiet and inspiring space to write for a few days or weeks to help you settle into your new surroundings.
