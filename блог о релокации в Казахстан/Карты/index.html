Palace of Peace and Reconciliation

A unique and impressive building located in the heart of Astana, the Palace of Peace and Reconciliation is a symbol of unity and peace. It has a concert hall and conference rooms and serves as a center for international and inter-religious dialogue.

Khan Shatyr Entertainment Center

An iconic landmark of Astana, the Khan Shatyr Entertainment Center is a huge tent-shaped building that offers a range of entertainment options, including shopping, dining, and entertainment facilities.

Astana Opera

One of the largest and most modern opera theaters in Central Asia, the Astana Opera is a stunning building that offers a variety of classical and contemporary performances and events.
